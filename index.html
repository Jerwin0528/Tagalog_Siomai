ABOUT US

We are the BSIT 1-D STUDENTS, TAGALOG, JERWIN H., ONDIS, THIRDEE C., SANCHEZ, JUSTINE JAMES G. AND ALMEDA, ARCHER MILES M.
Welcome to Siomai Rice On The Road, your ultimate destination for all things siomai rice! Our journey began with a simple love for this delicious and comforting Filipino dish. Siomai rice embodies the essence of Filipino street food culture - a delightful combination of savory siomai, fragrant rice, and flavorful sauces that captivate taste buds and bring joy with every bite.
At Siomai On The Road, we understand that food is more than just sustenance - it’s a gateway to connection, culture, and community. That’s why we invite you to join us on this gastronomic adventure as we explore the world of siomai rice together. Whether you’re seeking inspiration for your next meal or simply craving a taste of home, let SiomaiRice.com be your trusted companion on your culinary journey. Together, let’s savor the flavors, celebrate the traditions, and spread the joy of siomai rice far and wide.
Join us today and discover the endless possibilities that await you at Siomai On The Road!
